Design Review Center

Use NASA-style design reviews to prepare for project checkpoints and final presentations.

MCR

Mission Concept Review

Show that the problem, mission, users, requirements, and constraints are understood.

PDR

Preliminary Design Review

Compare concept options, justify the selected design, and identify major risks before building.

CDR

Critical Design Review

Prove that the design is ready for fabrication or final testing using CAD, calculations, drawings, and test plans.

FRR

Final Readiness Review

Confirm safety, materials, procedures, test setup, roles, and success criteria before the final mission.
