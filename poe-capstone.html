Final Mission

Planetary Rover Mission Challenge

Teams operate a VEX-based rover through a simulated Mars or lunar field. The rover must navigate terrain, complete a sample or payload task, collect mission evidence, and return to base or finish at a target zone.

Mission Tasks

Field Requirements

  • Navigate an obstacle or crater field
  • Use a sensor or programmed decision
  • Collect, move, or deploy a sample/payload
  • Protect critical rover subsystems
  • Document testing and redesign
Design Review

Final Presentation

  • Mission requirements matrix
  • Subsystem diagrams
  • Calculations and test data
  • Risk and safety analysis
  • Final recommendation for future missions

NASA-style design reviews

Teams complete Mission Concept Review, Preliminary Design Review, Critical Design Review, and Mission Readiness Review checkpoints throughout the year.
