Unit Project

Aerospace Manufacturing Documentation Package: Create a complete documentation package for an aerospace component such as a payload bracket, wing rib, sensor mount, fin jig, avionics housing, or fuselage panel support. Your package should be clear enough for another person to understand the part, make it, inspect it, and track revisions.
