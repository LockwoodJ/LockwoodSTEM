Course expectations and assessment structure for Principles of Engineering: Planetary Rover Systems.

Course Home
Course Purpose

Principles through a rover mission

Students study engineering systems, energy, statics, materials, mechanisms, fluid power, electronics, control systems, and design documentation through a year-long planetary rover pathway.

Class Expectations

What students do every week

  • Maintain an engineering notebook
  • Build and test safely with VEX or FabLab tools
  • Use data to justify design choices
  • Communicate work through design reviews
Major Assessments

How learning is shown

  • Unit engineering tasks
  • Rover subsystem projects
  • Notebook checks
  • Quizzes and skill checks
  • Final rover mission capstone
