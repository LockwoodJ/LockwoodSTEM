- `ied.html` now includes a section pointing students to the aerospace-focused IED project sequence.

- All new project pages use Lockwood STEM navigation, footer, colors, card styling, and accessibility-friendly contrast.

## Project Structure

Unit 1: Mission Patch / Academy Badge, Aerospace Component Reverse Engineering
Unit 2: Air Rocket Fin and Nose Cone System, Drone Payload Mount / Sensor Bracket
Unit 3: Astronaut / Pilot Assistive Product, Aerospace Ground Support Equipment
Unit 4: Mechanical Aerospace Automata, Aircraft Control Surface Mechanism
