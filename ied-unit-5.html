Start Unit 5 Here

Unit 5 shifts from documentation to physical performance. You will investigate material choices, load paths, strength-to-weight ratio, testing methods, failure analysis, and redesign through aerospace-inspired structures.

Unit Project

Lightweight Aerospace Structure Challenge: Design, build, test, and improve a lightweight aerospace structure such as a wing spar, fuselage frame, payload support, truss, or bracket. Use evidence from testing to justify redesign decisions.
