Unit 4

Mechanical Aerospace Automata

Students design and build a small mechanical display that uses physical mechanisms to show an aerospace motion such as launch, orbit, landing gear deployment, or flight control movement.

Fabrication Request
UnitUnit 4
Length12–18 class periods
ConnectionAerospace

Challenge Overview

This project is designed to connect engineering design skills to a realistic aerospace-inspired problem.

What You Are Designing

Students design and build a small mechanical display that uses physical mechanisms to show an aerospace motion such as launch, orbit, landing gear deployment, or flight control movement.

MechanismsMotionAerospace Display

Skills You Will Practice

Mechanisms, motion transfer, cams, gears, linkages, testing

Major Deliverables

Use these items to guide your project evidence and final submission.

Submit

  • Motion concept sketch
  • Mechanism diagram
  • CAD model of key parts
  • Prototype
  • Testing notes
  • Final automata
  • Motion transfer explanation

Fabrication Expectations

Laser cutting, 3D printing, cardboard, and hand-built prototypes may be used depending on certification and project needs.

Extension Challenges

  • Add multiple linked motions
  • Improve reliability through iteration
  • Create a display label explaining the mechanism
