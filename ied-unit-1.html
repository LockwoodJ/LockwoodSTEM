Unit Project

Aerospace Mission Concept Challenge: A research or emergency-response team needs an aerospace-based concept to deliver or protect a fragile sensor payload in a remote location after a natural disaster. Your team will create an early design package that defines the problem, compares possible concepts, and recommends one solution for future prototyping.
