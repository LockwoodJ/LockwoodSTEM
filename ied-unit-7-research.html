Learning Goal

Use this lesson page to understand the purpose, expectations, and student task for Research and Benchmarking.

  • Connect the task to your capstone mission need.
  • Use evidence to make design decisions.
  • Document the full design process from problem definition to final review.
  • Prepare for your final design review presentation.

Student Task

Complete the capstone activity assigned by your teacher. Focus on documenting the decision you made, the evidence that supports it, and the next step your team should take.

Submit or record: notebook evidence, required activity responses, sketches or screenshots when needed, and a short reflection explaining what you learned.

Notebook Prompts

Use these prompts when your teacher asks for a notebook entry or progress check.

Understand

What is the engineering purpose of today’s task?

Apply

How does this skill connect to an aerospace component, structure, system, or mission?

Evidence

What sketch, data, CAD screenshot, measurement, or test result supports your work?

Reflect

What would you improve, revise, or test next?

Key Idea

Engineers do not only create final products. They create evidence that explains why a design should work, how it can be made, and how it can be improved.
