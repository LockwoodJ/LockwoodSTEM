# Lockwood STEM IED Editable Templates

These files are designed to be uploaded to Google Drive and converted into Google Workspace formats.

## Files Included

- `Decision_Matrix_Template.xlsx`  
  Upload to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets. Students can score design concepts using weighted criteria.

- `Design_Review_Google_Slides_Template.pptx`  
  Upload to Google Drive and open with Google Slides. Students can copy and edit the presentation structure for design reviews.

- `CAD_File_Checklist_Template.xlsx`  
  Upload to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets. Students use this before submitting CAD files, screenshots, and exports.

- `BOM_Template.xlsx`  
  Upload to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets. Students track parts, quantities, fabrication methods, costs, and statuses.

- `Test_Data_Template.xlsx`  
  Upload to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets. Students record tests, calculate strength-to-weight, and summarize redesign decisions.

## Recommended Google Drive Setup

Create a folder named:

`IED Editable Templates`

Then upload all files. For each file:

1. Right-click the file in Google Drive.
2. Choose **Open with**.
3. Select **Google Sheets** or **Google Slides**.
4. Save the converted file as your master template.
5. Share a copy link with students using **Make a copy** links when possible.
