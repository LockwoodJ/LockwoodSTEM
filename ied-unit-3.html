Start Unit 3 Here

Unit 3 moves from hand sketches and technical drawings into 3D CAD modeling. You will use Fusion to create aerospace components with clear design intent, controlled dimensions, organized features, and documentation that prepares your part for review, prototyping, or fabrication.

Unit Project

Aerospace Component CAD Model: Model a part such as a payload bracket, UAV sensor mount, rocket fin alignment jig, wing rib, small avionics housing, or fuselage panel support. Your model should be dimensioned, editable, organized, and documented clearly enough for review or fabrication planning.
