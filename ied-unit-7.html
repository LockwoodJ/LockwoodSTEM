Start Unit 7 Here

Unit 7 is the capstone experience for Introduction to Engineering Design. You will define a mission need, research, sketch, model, document, prototype or simulate, test, redesign, and present a final aerospace-focused solution.

Overview

Start Here

Unit overview, capstone expectations, deliverables, pacing, and final design review requirements.

 Mission

Mission Brief

Choose or receive a mission scenario and identify the need, users, and problem context.

 Requirements

Requirements and Constraints

Translate the mission into measurable criteria, constraints, and success metrics.

 Research

Research and Benchmarking

Study existing solutions and summarize useful design patterns without copying.

 Concepts

Concept Development

Generate, sketch, compare, and select a promising design direction.

 CAD/Prototype

CAD and Prototype Plan

Create a model, prototype, or simulation plan that matches available tools and materials.

 Testing

Testing and Redesign

Collect evidence, analyze results, identify failures, and improve the design.

 Documentation

Final Documentation Package

Prepare drawings, BOM, process notes, test data, and revision evidence.

 Presentation

Final Design Review

Present and defend the solution using evidence from the full design process.

Unit 7 Essential Question

How do engineers use the full design process to develop, test, document, and defend a mission-based solution?

Unit 7 Deliverables

  • Mission brief
  • Problem statement
  • Requirements matrix
  • Research notes
  • Concept sketches
  • Decision matrix
  • CAD/prototype plan
  • Test plan and data
  • Redesign evidence
  • Final documentation package
  • Final design review presentation
