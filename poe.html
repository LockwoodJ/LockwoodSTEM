Course Story

Engineering a planetary exploration rover

Principles of Engineering is rebuilt around a Mars/Lunar rover mission. The rover is the context; the engineering topics remain the core of the course.

VEX Platform

Build, test, program, and improve

VEX robotics is used throughout the course to model real rover subsystems: drivetrain, chassis, sensors, sample collection, pneumatics, and autonomous control.

Aerospace Pathway

NASA-style systems engineering

Students complete requirements, trade studies, design reviews, testing, verification, and a final mission readiness presentation.
