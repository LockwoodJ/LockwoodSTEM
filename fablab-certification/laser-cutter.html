Advanced Equipment

Laser Cutter Certification: OMTech 80W / 40W

Laser cutters are powerful tools for precise cutting and engraving. Safe laser work begins with approved materials, correct setup, ventilation, test settings, and constant monitoring.

Equipment You Will Use

OMTech 80W Laser

Large-format CO₂ laser used for cutting and engraving approved materials.

OMTech 40W Laser

CO₂ laser used for smaller engraving and cutting jobs.

What You Need to Learn

The laser does exactly what your file and settings tell it to do.

Your job is to prepare clean vector/raster artwork, choose approved materials, focus correctly, and test speed and power before running the final project.

  • Use only approved laser-safe materials.
  • Separate cut, score, and engrave operations clearly.
  • Confirm ventilation and air assist before starting.
  • Run test settings on scrap when appropriate.
  • Stay with the machine until the job is complete.
Never cut unknown plastics, PVC, vinyl, or reflective materials. If you are not sure what a material is, do not put it in the laser.

Student Procedure

  1. Confirm the material is approved and flat on the bed.
  2. Prepare the file using the class convention for cut, score, and engrave operations.
  3. Turn on ventilation/air assist and check the bed for scraps.
  4. Focus the laser using the approved focus method.
  5. Run a small test if settings are uncertain.
  6. Monitor the full job and stop immediately for flame, heavy smoke, or unexpected motion.
