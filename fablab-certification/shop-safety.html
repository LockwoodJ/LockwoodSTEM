Shop Safety & Emergency Procedures

The FabLab is a shared workspace. Your first responsibility is to protect yourself, your classmates, the equipment, and the learning environment.

What You Need to Learn

Safety is a habit, not a checklist.

Before you use any tool, pause and scan the workspace. Ask: Do I have the right PPE? Is the material secure? Is the tool in good condition? Do I know what to do if something goes wrong?

  • Wear required PPE before entering active work zones.
  • Secure hair, sleeves, lanyards, jewelry, and loose clothing.
  • Inspect tools before use and report damage immediately.
  • Know where emergency stops, exits, first aid, fire extinguisher, and eyewash are located.
  • Use only tools you are certified and approved to use.
Stop-work rule: Any student may pause work when they notice an unsafe condition. Safety is never considered an interruption.

Emergency Habits

  1. Stop the tool or step away from the hazard if it is safe to do so.
  2. Notify the instructor immediately.
  3. Keep other students away from the area.
  4. Do not restart work until the instructor clears the situation.
