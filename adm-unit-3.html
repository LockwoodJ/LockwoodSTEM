Vector design, kerf, material thickness, engraving/cutting workflows, file prep, and assembly planning.

Unit Learning

Focus on skills, evidence, technical accuracy, and engineering decisions.

Learning Targets

By the end of this unit, students should be able to explain the concepts, apply the tools, and document their engineering process.

Understand

Core Concepts

Explain the unit vocabulary, systems, relationships, and engineering decisions in context.

Apply

Technical Skills

Use appropriate tools, calculations, models, tests, or fabrication workflows to solve problems.

Document

Engineering Evidence

Record sketches, data, screenshots, photos, failures, revisions, and conclusions in the notebook.

Student Deliverables

  • Engineering notebook entries with dates, evidence, and reflections.
  • Technical work showing calculations, models, sketches, or CAD as needed.
  • Prototype, test, analysis, or design decision evidence.
  • Final reflection explaining what changed and why.

Project Connection

This unit supports the course project work by building the technical skills students need to design, test, document, and improve engineered solutions.
